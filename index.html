About Nautical Nemo

Welcome to Nautical Nemo Swimming Pool. We run a community, non-profit organization that focuses on the dvelopment and recreation of swimmers in our locale. With a 25m pool, a 50m pool, a hot tub and sauna, a swirl pool, a shallow end and a kiddie pool, our complex can accompany anyone. There are women's, men's and family changerooms to fit anyone's needs. Our lifeguards have completed all necessary requirements and are the finest in the city! Please browse our Schedule and Prices to complete the Nautical experience!
